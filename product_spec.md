# Restoration AI - Product Master Specification

## Core Value Proposition
Restoration AI is an AI-native receptionist, dispatch, and marketing system built specifically for the property restoration industry (water, fire, mold). It ensures no emergency call is missed, bad leads are filtered, and operations run efficiently.

## 1. Call Handling & Intake (The AI Receptionist)
- **Intake Protocols:** Verifies property owner/decision maker, gathers incident summary, identifies source of damage, and logs affected areas.
- **Rapid Disqualification:** Identifies non-leads (tenants, solicitors, out-of-area) and gracefully ends calls to save on Lead/Ad spend.
- **Customization:** Add specific services provided (and not provided), custom pricing, and business knowledge.
- **Multi-Channel:** Support for multiple phone numbers with unique routing rules (e.g., Google Ads line vs. Main Office line).

## 2. Dispatch & Routing (The Engine)
- **24/7 Cascade Dispatch:** Can sequentially contact team members based on priority ranking until a job is accepted.
- **Role-Based Routing:** Dispatch specifically to Mitigation PMs, Crew Chiefs, Assistant Techs, or Estimators.
- **Multi-Tier Dispatch:** Can dispatch a PM immediately, and automatically queue a crew to arrive later.
- **Digital Work Authorizations:** AI automatically texts custom work authorization contracts to callers for instant digital signature before arrival.

## 3. Team & Operational Management
- **Smart Scheduling:** Routes appointments based on priority ranking, round-robin, or existing team routes.
- **Multi-Office/Territory:** Admins can manage multiple branches, defining specific zip codes and service areas per branch.
- **Full Calendar Sync:** Internal calendar view for managing all appointments and dispatches.

## 4. Dashboard & Analytics
- **Call Deep-Dives:** Click any call to view caller ID, listen to the audio recording, read the AI-generated summary, and read the full text transcript.
- **Lead Tracking (Leaky Bucket Prevention):** Incomplete calls are flagged as "Unreviewed Leads" for manual follow-up. Users review them daily and click "Mark Reviewed".
- **Outcome Tagging:** Calls are auto-tagged (e.g., Lead Captured - Emergency, Lead Captured - Inspection).

## 5. Marketing & Reputation (Review Engine)
- **Smart Review Requests:** Sends SMS review requests using personalized AI images (e.g., a photo of the company van with text overlay "Hey [Customer First Name]!").
- **Review Filtering:** Optional setting to intercept negative feedback. Good ratings go to Google; bad ratings go to an internal feedback form.
- **Website Voice Chat:** A widget that allows website visitors to instantly speak with the AI dispatcher via voice.

## 6. Customer Experience
- **Automated Communication:** Instant SMS appointment confirmations, 24-hour reminders, and 1-hour reminders.
- **Pre-Arrival Intelligence:** Sends a secure link for the customer to upload photos of the damage, which are saved directly to the job file.
