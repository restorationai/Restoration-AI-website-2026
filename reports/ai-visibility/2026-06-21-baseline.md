**Metric:** % of tracked buyer prompts whose AI answer *mentions* Restoration AI (mention rate), and % that *cite* restorationai.io (citation rate).

## Insights
- The **AI-receptionist-for-restoration** answer is wide open: ChatGPT is inventing brand names because no authoritative, well-cited source exists. First mover with strong, cited content can own it.
- The **leads** answer cites real competitor blogs. Goal = displace them with better-cited, more specific content (our "get more leads" Pillar B posts).
- Both answers reward the same things our posts now do: clear answer capsules, FAQ schema, citations, identifiable author. The gap is simply **coverage + authority over time**.
