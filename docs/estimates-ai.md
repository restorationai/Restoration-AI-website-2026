# Estimates AI — Product Reference

> Internal reference for blog post writing. Not yet public. Coming soon.

Estimates AI is a separate product from the AI Answering suite. It handles the full estimate lifecycle for restoration contractors — from first look at a job to final payment collected. Five core features:

---

## 1. Smart Estimate
AI generates a full line-item restoration estimate from job details and damage photos. Image recognition identifies damage types and materials automatically. Contractor reviews, edits, and approves. Outputs a professional PDF with digital signature capture.

**Dream outcome:** Never start an estimate from scratch again. Submit faster, look more professional, win more jobs.

---

## 2. Estimate Audit
Upload any existing estimate and AI scans it for missed line items based on job type and industry standards. Shows exactly what was missed and the dollar amount left on the table. One-click to add missing items back in.

**Dream outcome:** Stop leaving money behind. Catch every missed item before you submit.

---

## 3. Estimate Defense
When an adjuster cuts or denies line items, AI generates evidence-based rebuttals citing IICRC standards, building codes, and Xactimate pricing guides. Produces a professional defense document ready to send. Tracks dispute outcomes and recovery rate over time.

**Dream outcome:** Stop accepting adjuster cuts. Win back money that's rightfully yours.

---

## 4. Supplement Writer *(NEW)*
When hidden damage is discovered mid-job, AI generates new line items and writes the justification narrative explaining why the additional scope is necessary. Outputs as a separate supplement document linked to the original estimate.

**Dream outcome:** Document scope changes instantly. Get paid for every dollar of work you actually do.

---

## 5. Claims Tracker
Tracks every claim from FNOL to final payment. Kanban and list views. Automated reminders when claims stall. Payment tracking (ACV, holdbacks, supplements). All documents, photos, and correspondence stored per claim. Natively connected to all other Restoration AI features.

**Dream outcome:** Never lose track of a claim again. Know exactly where every job stands and who owes you money.
