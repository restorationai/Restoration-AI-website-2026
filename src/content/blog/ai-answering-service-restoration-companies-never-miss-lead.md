## How Does an AI Answering Service Actually Qualify a Restoration Lead?

A well-trained AI doesn't just take a name and number. It asks the right questions: What type of damage? Residential or commercial property? Has an insurance claim been filed? Is there standing water right now? This triage separates a $2,000 job from a $50,000 one before you even call back.

Here's what a real call flow looks like.

A homeowner calls at 11:30 PM. Your AI answers on the first ring. It greets them by your company name. It asks what happened. The caller says there's water coming through the ceiling from a burst pipe upstairs.

The AI collects the address. It asks if they've already filed a claim with their insurance company. It asks whether there's standing water and how many rooms are affected. It tells the caller that a technician will be in touch within the hour. It sends you, or your on-call tech, a full dispatch alert with every detail captured.

You wake up at 7 AM to a summary of every call that came in overnight. Job type, severity level, contact info, insurance status, and the caller's urgency. You know exactly which jobs to prioritize before you've had your first cup of coffee.
